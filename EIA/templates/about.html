About Eastern Investgative Agency

The Eastern Investigative Agency (EIA) operates independently and plays an important role in the service of our country in order to make a crime free society. EIA is the premier investigative agency in the country today, with a dual responsibility to investigate grievous cases and provide leadership and direction in fighting corruption to the Police force across the country.

Our Mission is to uphold the law of the land through in-depth investigation and successful prosecution of offences; to provide leadership and direction to Police Forces working with us and to act as the Nodal Agency for enhancing interstate and international cooperation in law enforcement.

We have taken urgent step and investigation for various types of crime and will be continuing to do so. We have even implemented various types of new tech like face-recognition to aid us in our investigation.
