Welcome to Eastern Investigative Agency!


This is the official website of EIA. We at EIA are commited to ensure your safety always and strongly believe that together we will be able to create a crime free society.

Who Are We?
EIA (Eastern Investigative Agency)

The EIA functioning under the department of Personnel, Ministry of Personnel and Public Grievances, is the premiere investigating agency of the eastern part of the country. It is an elite force playing a major role in the preservation of values in public life and in ensuring the health of the national economy. It is also the nodal police agency of the eastern part of the country which coordinates investigation on behalf of Interpol Member countries as well.

Know more about us here:  About Us
How do we help you?

You can file reports with us directly through this website by simply logging in. This report shall be verified by us and all the updates on your report shall be posted which you can track easily. You can attach pictures and video files as well which will help us in investigating your case. We run matches in our database aginst the pictures and videos uploaded and in case of a match, you will be sent an alert immediately.
How can you help us?

You can help us find missing individuals by simply logging in and then then after clicking on the missing help card, you will be redirected to a page wherein you will have to upload a photo of the missing person along with his/her name and your details as well. If the photo is a match against those in our database, we will be alerted.
Steps For Users:

Step 1: Click on the login/register button.
Step 2: Select from the Citizen/Employee options.
Step 3: If you do not have an account, register yourself first and then login into that account.
Step 4: After logging in, you can select from the various options available.
Step 5: Make sure to logout from your account once you are done.
